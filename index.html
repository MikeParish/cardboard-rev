What is Cardboard Revolution?

Cardboard Revolution is a worker-owned design cooperative. We're currently working on numerous exciting projects, with plans for design, development, and publishing. Check out our works in progress, download print and plays of some of our prototypes, and read about our fantastic team.

Featured Projects

Mononoke

Mononoke is a semi-cooperative bluffing game where players take the role of heroes defending a cursed castle from bizarre spirits in feudal Japan.
Learn more.

Animals

Animals is a collaborative puzzle game that focuses on spatial reasoning.
Learn more.

Pest Quest

Pest Quest is an asymmetrical cooperative game teaching basic concepts of Integrated Pest Management, or IPM, in an entomological context.
Learn more.

Life for the Loam

Life for the Loam is a competitive card game teaching the role of animals and microbes in maintaining healthy soils.
Learn more.
